ABOUT US

Welcome to LOVEATALY, a successful event catering and food delivery company based in Middlesbrough, operating throughout Teesside.

We specialize in catering for a wide range of events and special occasions, delivering healthy breakfasts, mid-morning snacks, and lunches to offices and businesses, as well as providing mobile catering solutions for events, parties, and celebrations.

Our aim is to provide friendly and flexible service every day Monday to Friday.

If you want us to call to your workplace, give us a ring and we’ll be more than happy to please you with our products.
